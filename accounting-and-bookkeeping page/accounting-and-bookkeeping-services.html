    Data & Security

    Strict Data Security
    Measures

    At Axiom World, we believe that we are liable for the client’s data security & privacy herefore we take precautionary measures to make sure optimum security is maintained.

    We Ensure:

    • Encryption of all in transit data
    • Tight monitoring of sending/receiving data
    • Physical security controls to lessen the risk of data leaving the facility via recording devices, cameras and hard copies (Paperless work floor)
    • Non-Disclosure Agreements (NDA) signed by employees with stringent data security clauses, punishable under law
    • Disabled USB, CD, DVD, floppy drive, SCSI, parallel or any of the other ports
    • No or restricted fax and printing capabilities at processing sites
    • Network security is maintained by help of antivirus and firewall protection
    • Segmented LAN with firewall protection while transferring data from client’s office to our office.
    • Regular backups of server hosting the data
